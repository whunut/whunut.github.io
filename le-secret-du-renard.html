Farewell, said the fox. Here is my secret. It is very simple: we only see cleary with the heart. The essential is invisible to the eyes.

The essential is invisible to the eyes, repeated the little prince, in order that he remember.

It is the time you spend with your rose that makes your rose so important.

It is the time that I spend with my rose...said the little prince, in order that he remember.

Men have forgotten this truth, said the fox. But you must not forget it. You muse become responsible always of what you have tamed. You are responsible for the rose...

I am responsible for my rose...repeated the little prince, in order that he remember.
